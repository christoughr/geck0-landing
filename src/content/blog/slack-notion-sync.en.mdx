## The problem is not the tools — it is the silos

Most teams already picked Slack for chat and Notion for docs. The pain is **searching three places** for one answer.

## What geck0 does

- **Ingest** from connectors (Notion token, uploads, and more on the roadmap)
- **Link** related snippets in a knowledge graph
- **Answer** in natural language with citations back to the source

You keep editing in Notion; geck0 reads and answers.

## Practical tip

Start with one high-churn channel or wiki space, measure time-to-answer for new hires, then expand.
