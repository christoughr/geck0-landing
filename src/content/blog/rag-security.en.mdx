## Why security matters before you connect sources

Teams want fast answers, but **access control must follow the source system**. If someone cannot read a Notion page today, geck0 should not surface it in Q&A tomorrow.

## Three checks we recommend

1. **Workspace isolation** — each customer tenant gets its own knowledge store and API keys.
2. **Audit trail** — log who asked what and which documents were cited.
3. **Rate limits + bot protection** — protect public forms and APIs from abuse.

## geck0 defaults

- Session-gated beta app at `app.geck0.ai`
- Optional Turnstile on marketing forms
- Distributed rate limiting when Redis/KV is configured
