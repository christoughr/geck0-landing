## 1. Confirm waitlist / invite

Check for your app.geck0.ai invite email. If you are still waiting, [join the waitlist](/#contact) on the homepage.

## 2. Connect Slack, Notion, Drive

We request read-only OAuth scopes. For locked workspaces, loop in IT before connect day.

## 3. Wait for first sync (~15 min)

The knowledge graph fills after connect. Watch sync status on the `/app` dashboard.

## 4. Try three real questions

Ask questions your team repeats. Verify source links on every answer.

## 5. Workspace & seats

Invite teammates under Settings → Team. Beta may cap seats per workspace.

## 6. Share /demo with stakeholders

No login required — use the [demo](/demo) video and interactive preview to align buyers or execs.

## 7. Weekly feedback

Send wrong or missing answers to hello@geck0.ai so we can improve indexing and retrieval.
