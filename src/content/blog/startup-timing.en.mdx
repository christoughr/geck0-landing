## At 20 people: the first “same question again” signal

Once you pass ~20 people, **onboarding cost** spikes. If three senior engineers repeat the same explanations for five hours each week, that is 60 hours/month — a hidden cost that adds up fast.

geck0 Starter can address much of this at a fraction of that cost.

## At 50 people: knowledge loss begins

Has a key member leaving ever caused a **two-week project delay**? Past 50 people, **knowledge archiving** becomes a survival issue.

## At 100 people: you need Insight Pulse

At 100 people, no CEO can track everything. geck0 **Insight Pulse** detects risk signals and opportunities from accumulated patterns automatically.
