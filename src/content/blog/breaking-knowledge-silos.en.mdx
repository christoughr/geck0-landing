## 1. Record questions, not just answers

The same question repeats because **answers are not captured**. Slack threads, meeting notes, 1:1 docs — make Q&A searchable.

## 2. Manage document lifetimes

Stale docs are noise, not knowledge. Run a quarterly "is this still valid?" review.

## 3. Connect tools — don't rip and replace

Instead of migrating Notion to Confluence, add a **connection layer** on top of what you already use. That is what geck0 does.

## 4. Treat onboarding conversations as data

A new hire's first-two-weeks question list is a **map of knowledge gaps**. Analyze it to see where the org is blind.

## 5. Make content readable before asking AI

Q&A quality follows **source quality**. Structured docs, clear titles, metadata — that is how geck0 reaches 94% on internal benchmarks.
