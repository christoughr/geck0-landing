## The problem: enterprise AI without hallucinations

Generic ChatGPT does not know your internal data. Simple RAG can still produce **answers without evidence**.

geck0's AI Q&A Engine addresses this with three verification layers.

## Layer 1: Knowledge Graph Retrieval

Not just vector search — **relationship-aware retrieval**. Ask about "Project A decisions" and geck0 pulls related Slack threads, Notion pages, and Jira tickets as a **connected graph**.

## Layer 2: Source Attribution

Every answer includes **links to source documents**. Users can click through and verify.

## Layer 3: Confidence Scoring

If confidence is below 70%, geck0 says "not sure" and escalates to the right owner.

## Results

**94% accuracy** on our internal benchmark (500 questions), average response time 2.1s.
